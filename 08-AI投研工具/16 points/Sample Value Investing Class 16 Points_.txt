Sample Value Investing Class 16 Points:
Please answer in GREAT DETAIL 
Spelling out the entire rationale for your answer.
Product:
1. Does the Product have:
- A value proposition to all constituencies that is unique and easy to understand?
- A stable recurring revenue base.
- Products and services that are highly unlikely to commoditized?
2. Does the company  have a large market potential for the product with the opportunity to grow for the foreseeable future?
3. Is there a definable competitive advantage (Either in culture, product, market share, etc.) in a business niche that we can understand?  
If there IS a competitive advantage, what is it and what makes it unique and sustainable?
Sales and Marketing:
4. Does this company have an above average sales and marketing organization?
Financial/Accounting:
5. Historical Financials
· What do the historical financials tell us, are there any meaningful trends?
· What does the financial history tell you about the business and your ability to forecast it?
· Is this a business that currently generates, or in the future will generate, significant Economic Value Added (EVA)?  Calculate current  EVA?
· What are the financial characteristics of a great business?
And what is all the evidence that that this company has (or does not have) the financial characteristics of a potentially great business?
· What are the unit economics if determinable?
6. Accounting
· is the accounting and financial disclosure both complete and easy to understand?
· Is it easy to determine the “OWNERS EARNINGS” (see THE ESSAYS OF WARREN BUFFETT. PG 197)?  If so what are they specifically, and are they different than the GAAP accounting earnings, and why?
· What do you do with this owner’s earnings number now that you have it?
7. Is there a high probability of either increasing margins, or at worst mainting them through a variety of potential business environments?
8. Does the company have good controls and cost analysis?
Culture and Management:
9. Does top management have a clear vision of what the company can be “best in the world at” 
(This point is written about in Good To Great)?  If they have a clear vision, are they intensely focused on it?
10. Does the company have depth to its management?
11.  Is there an appropriately focused entrepreneurial culture that is both unique and sustainable?  (ie can they get things done?)
12. Management orientation:
· Are they a management team that demonstrably thinks and acts like owners?
· Does top management have a demonstrated understanding that the financial purpose of a public company it to build EVA
(or something that is the equivalent of EVA) in per share terms?
· Do they have a thoughtful, shareholder oriented compensation structure throughout the company?
13. Are they a management team with unquestionable integrity and business ethics?  
Do they avoid self-dealings and any improper behavior?
14. Do they have outstanding employee relations?
Does the company have outstanding employee relations?
General
15. Is there anything else unique to this company that should be considered?
16. Your valuation.  (REMEMBER WHAT THE VALUE OF AN ASSET IS)
 Is this company a potentially attractive or unattractive as an investment?  In detail show why.
